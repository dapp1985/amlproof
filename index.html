// FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about AML wallet verification.

What does address verification show? ▾
Address verification shows whether a crypto wallet has been associated with any illicit activities or is on any sanctions lists.
How does the Trust Wallet connection work? ▾
We use WalletConnect v2. Click "Connect Wallet", scan the QR with Trust Wallet. No private keys are ever shared — only your public address is read.
What do the risk parameters mean? ▾
Risk indicators include sanctions status, dark market exposure, stolen coin connections, and more. Each scored as a percentage — higher means greater risk.
How fast is the balance replenished? ▾
Balance replenishment typically occurs within minutes after a transaction is confirmed on the blockchain.
