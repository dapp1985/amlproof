// FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about AML wallet verification.

What does address verification show? ▾
Address verification shows whether a crypto wallet has been associated with any illicit activities or is on any sanctions lists. It helps ensure you're not transacting with risky addresses.
How does the Trust Wallet connection work? ▾
We use WalletConnect v2 — click "Connect Wallet", scan the QR code with Trust Wallet. No private keys are ever shared — only your public address is read.
What do the risk parameters mean? ▾
Risk indicators include sanctions status, dark market exposure, stolen coin connections, and more. Each is scored as a percentage — higher means greater risk.
How fast is the balance replenished? ▾
Balance replenishment typically occurs within minutes after a transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, depending on network congestion.
